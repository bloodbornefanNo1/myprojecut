Hollow knight Pale Court wiki

Hollow Knight Pale Court is a Hollow knight mod that ads 5 new bosses, 4 new amulets and one pantheon that challenges player to beat every boss from the mod. Also ads new location that looks like path of pain.


Bosses:

  • Fierce Dryya
  • kindly Isma
  • Mighty Hegemol
  • Mysterious Ze'mer
  • Tiso

Trivia:

New bosses are The Five Great Knights in their most powerfull form
